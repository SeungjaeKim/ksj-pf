Squirrel Rescue Brigade

Catch falling squirrels across seven playable lanes, then auto-load them into the fixed ambulance dock on lane eight. Random supply drops give the run short, readable power spikes.

Desktop Left / Right one lane at a time
Mobile Drag the trampoline across the route
Goal Catch 7 times, then bounce into lane 8
Danger 5 misses ends the run
How To Play
  • Squirrels spawn at random 5 to 7 second intervals instead of waiting for the previous rescue to finish.
  • Each bounce climbs to a random high arc near the apartment top, then bends back toward the next target lane.
  • The 7th catch launches the squirrel into a final lane 8 ambulance bounce, and rescue only scores on arrival.
  • Supply drops appear every 10 to 15 seconds. The wide trampoline power-up now covers two lanes at once.

Concept

Seven lanes plus one dock

The trampoline only plays inside seven clear lanes, while the ambulance lives in its own eighth dock so the ending read is much cleaner.

Feel

High arcs, guided landings

Every bounce now picks a different high apex near the building roof, then converges toward the next lane so the motion feels lively without becoming random noise.

Presentation

Power-ups are physical drops

Power-ups no longer trigger invisibly. They appear as real falling pickups on the stage and activate only if the trampoline catches them.
